Our Vision

The promise of the cloud is to make data available to every individual in any place in the world.CommuFi Network is an umbrela project under SkyLabase with the aim to provide broadband internet connectivity to the community. We believe in the saying that internet connectivity equals opportunity. The migration from 2nd generation networks to 3rd and 4th generation networks in our community is an opportunity to boost our economy.The opportunities are endless, ranging from eLearning, eCommerce, Video on Demand(VoD). Connected devices in our homes cannot be forgotten, smart Tvs, smart kitchen cookers, driveless cars, drones and many more. It is there fore imperative to cease this opportunity and partake in the digital era of this Android Generation.
